Rhetoric and Dialectic

The art of reasoning consisted of various skills: speaking, reading and writing Latin, being able to speak in front of an audience, learning how to build sound arguments and develop the power to convince. How were these skills trained in the medieval classroom? Which texts were used and how did they form a coherent programme?
